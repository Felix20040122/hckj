All that stuff about huachekj

"huachekeji" project is an integrated technology based on LDH material composite modification. Combined with the characteristics of the developed materials, the textile dyeing and finishing wastewater treatment supporting process system is developed. The project has been awarded by Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications in terms of product development and technology application. Supported by Chongqing University, Chongqing Environmental Science Academy and other units, with the Chinese Academy of Engineering academician Xian Xuefu, The support and affirmation of industry leaders such as Ye Bin, chairman of Sichuan Jinshang Environmental Protection, ensure the high quality of the project, High standard completion, give full play to the environmental and economic benefits of the project.



The project has been committed to research and development of LDH matrix composite materials for a long time, and designed composite modification of LDH materials. The technical route of acid activation, and the integration of the characteristics of the developed water purification agent, the development of less power consumption, Maintaining simple wastewater treatment technology has good environmental and economic benefits. It has low cost, High performance, universal can effectively solve the problem of printing and dyeing wastewater treatment, It also conforms to the concept of harmonious coexistence between man and nature.
