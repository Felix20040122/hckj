Background

On October 28, 2023, the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology, the National Development and Reform Commission, the Ministry of Commerce, and the Municipal Administration of Market Supervision and Regulation issued a notice on the implementation plan for textile industry quality Upgrading (2023~2025).It is clearly pointed out that The green, low-carbon and circular development system has been improved, the consumption of energy and water resources per unit of industrial added value has been further reduced, the emission intensity of major pollutants has been continuously reduced, and the quality and scale of recycling of waste textiles have been continuously improved.

In order to implement the spirit of the Party's 20th National Congress and the Central Economic Work Conference, implement the relevant requirements of the "14th Five-Year Plan" for high-quality Development of Manufacturing industry, and promote the stable growth and high-quality development of the textile industry, this implementation plan is formulated.

it is necessary to enhance the toughness and competitiveness of textile products printing and dyeing processing. Optimize the development environment of the printing and dyeing industry, support printing and dyeing enterprises to carry out intelligent green transformation, reduce energy consumption and water consumption and pollutant emissions, and strengthen the complete advantages of the textile industry system and the development toughness of the industrial chain.

The textile industry is an important consumer goods industry, its components are important traditional pillar industries, livelihood industries and international advantages industries, and plays an important role in beautifying people's lives, serving economic development, achieving public prosperity, and enhancing cultural self-confidence.



Textile printing and dyeing wastewater pollution has been among the best in China's industrial industry. Its wastewater discharge accounts for about 5.6% of the country's wastewater discharge, 1.5 to 1.8 billion tons per year. It accounts for more than 70% of the wastewater of the textile industry. The market size of textile wastewater treatment industry will reach 20 billion in 2020, With the gradual tightening of environmental protection policies for enterprises in the industry, and the promotion of the construction of textile industry parks around the country, As well as policy guidance, the penetration rate of printing and dyeing wastewater treatment will increase rapidly, and the market size is expected to reach 45 billion in 2025.

Chemical oxygen demand (COD) emissions are about 240-300,000 tons per year, and the whole industry accounts for about 9%. As a major water polluter, the printing and dyeing industry ranks second and fourth in the national industrial sector in terms of wastewater discharge and total pollutants.



Under the background of strengthening policy standards and legal supervision, the traditional textile dyeing and finishing wastewater treatment technology is difficult due to the complexity of treatment. The problem of high water consumption has been unable to meet the requirements of treatment standards. At present, the single technology treatment on the market is faced with poor treatment effect Unable to meet the national permitted emission reuse standards; Although some treatment processes have good results, the investment cost is high High energy consumption and expensive maintenance. Therefore, the market is in urgent need of low-cost and efficient water purification agents and supporting treatment processes.

Based on the above background, under the guidance of the idea of national ecological civilization construction and synergistic effect of pollution reduction and carbon reduction, Independently developed LDH with high removal adsorption efficiency and moderate cost (Layered double hydroxides) Based on printing and dyeing wastewater treatment water purification agent and related technology.
