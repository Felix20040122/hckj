ABOUT US

huachekeji

Huache Technology project is an integrated technology based on LDH material composite modification. It has been supported by various academic institutions and industry leaders. The project focuses on developing a textile dyeing and finishing wastewater treatment process system. The LDH matrix composite materials and acid activation techniques are used to design a water purifier with low power consumption and simple maintenance. This technology provides a cost-effective and high-performance solution for treating printing and dyeing wastewater, aligning with the concept of harmonious coexistence between humans and nature.
