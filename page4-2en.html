Team profile

Chongqing Chuche Technology Co., Ltd. focuses on printing and dyeing wastewater treatment industry。The company is committed toSolve problems at home and abroadLimitations of printing and dyeing wastewater treatment technology in terms of purification cost and treatment efficiency,，Actively put into textile dyeing and finishing sewage treatment industry.The LDH based water purifier materials developed by the company have high efficiency adsorption and removal performance, and cooperate with biochemical treatment technology to optimize treatment It can effectively optimize the governance effect, and has good environmental protection and economic benefits. The company is committed to providing more and better quality for the people Products and services for the "green printing and dyeing" industry.


Members of this team cover materials, physics, automation, data science, communications and Financial Engineering and other majors, is a cross-disciplinary integration, full of innovative student teams.At the same time, the team is coordinated by Chongqing University of Posts and Telecommunications, Chongqing University and Chongqing Jiaotong University In collaboration with the Southwest Branch of the Chinese Academy of Environmental Sciences, To carry out close scientific research exchanges and cooperation, Professor Yuxin Zhang of Chongqing University, Zhu Kangwen, Senior Engineer, Chongqing Jiaotong University, and Feng Li, Senior engineer, Director of Water Environment Engineering Technology Innovation Center, Southwest Branch, Chinese Academy of Environmental Sciences Exchange and guidance on relevant research of this project from time to time, Ensure the progress of project research under the guidance of professional perspective Complete project planning smoothly and with high quality.


The company adheres to the development concept of independent research and development, bold innovation, strict product quality control, Committed to solving the problems of high cost, low efficiency and poor environmental protection of textile printing and dyeing water purification materials, To provide users with lower cost, higher efficiency, more environmentally friendly water purification products. In terms of management mode, the company is to put talents, meet user needs of creativity, A platform for effective combination of various internal and external resources to generate value, In addition, flat management enhances interaction and rapid response with users, Business results sharing, so that the organization is full of vitality, but also stimulate the creativity and motivation of employees. The company promises that it will always take technological innovation, product development and service perfection as its business philosophy. To provide users with more effective protection.
