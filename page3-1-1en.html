High performance

Product -- LDH based water purifier 1 takes advantage of LDH's unique layered structure, intercalability and anion exchange characteristics. The composite modification technical route is designed to realize the dual adsorption and removal of anions and ions. While improving the specific surface area of the composite material, Further enhance the purification and separation efficiency of dyes, heavy metal ions and N and P in dyeing water under high alkaline condition. Based on the treatment characteristics of the prepared water purification agent, the mixed multistage treatment process was developed and the process parameters were determined. Combined with the technical and economic comparison, the optimal treatment scheme is selected and continuously optimized until it reaches the practical standard.




Based on the characteristics of high N and P content, heavy metal ion concentration and COD concentration in printing and dyeing wastewater, Combined with the synergistic effect of LDH water purifier and biological treatment, the organic combination of biochemical and physicochemical effects is realized. A set of LDH-based water purifier - BAF-Fenton oxidation process with more efficient treatment efficiency was designed and developed. It is composed of physical and chemical pretreatment system, biochemical treatment system and advanced treatment system. The power consumption of the process is small, the maintenance is simple, and the environmental protection and economic benefits are good.
