Product mechanism

This team is based on LDH layered structure design, using hydrotalcite material with layered structure, interlayer ions, It has the characteristic of exchangeability, Using the intercalability and interlayer ion exchangeability of the layered compound subject under the action of strongly polar molecules, Introducing some functional guest material into the interlayer space and stretching the lamellar distance to form a cambium column compound, Increase the specific surface area of the composite material, promote the infiltration and ion transport of the material and solution, and improve the adsorption rate and adsorption capacity; At the same time, the influence mechanism, regulation theory and long-term mechanism of application of LDH-based water purification materials were studied. Form a low-cost, large-scale preparation method system for the stabilization and homogenization of composite materials, The disposal of difficult printing and dyeing wastewater and subsequent sludge provides cheap, Multi-purpose non-metallic mineral material system and efficient technology integration, and in Chongqing key chemical enterprises, Wastewater treatment enterprises in large industrial parks have demonstrated the application of printing and dyeing wastewater with high N content and wastewater containing heavy metal ions.



It is difficult to apply the traditional wastewater treatment method when the COD of wastewater is higher than 800mg/L in the actual printing and dyeing wastewater treatment project. When the concentration of heavy metal ions reaches 100ppm, The cost of single adsorption treatment increases sharply. BAF and Fenton oxidation processes are used in conjunction with LDH water purification materials. In the application of printing and dyeing wastewater treatment engineering has shown a high treatment effect, And with its simple processing process and low application cost, it has a very cost-effective technology. The primary processing system can play a good role in peak reduction; The secondary biochemical system has obvious removal of COD, NH3-N, TN and TP. Strong impact load resistance and stable effluent quality; The advanced treatment system can further degrade the refractory COD in water. LDH based water purifier -BAF- Fenton oxidation process can achieve low cost and high efficiency of wastewater purification. The combined technology has been well applied and popularized.
