Low consumption

Due to the layered structural characteristics and cost-effectiveness of LDH materials, it becomes feasible to significantly reduce the preparation cost of adsorbents for high-nitrogen,phosphorus, and heavy metal ions removal.

The collaborative development of bioremediation processes and LDH-based adsorbents harnesses the efficient adsorption and removal performance of LDH-based water purification materials, synergistically optimizing the treatment effectiveness of bioremediation techniques. This establishes an integrated technological system,LDH-based water purification agent-BAF-Fenton oxidation process, achieving a synergy greater than the sum of its parts. By adopting the composite process of this project, the treatment cost per ton of wastewater can typically be reduced by over 40%. Multiple practical applications in wastewater treatment have demonstrated the technical superiority and cost-effectiveness of this project.
