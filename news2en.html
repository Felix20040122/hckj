The team successfully obtained the qualification to help and started a new journey

On January 3, 2024, after full preparation, The team participated in the final of the 7th "YouChuangYougang" college Student Entrepreneurship Support Program in Chongqing and successfully obtained the qualification of help. For the future development of a new journey. This is the good news that team members and supporters have been waiting for. After months of hard work and preparation, the team finally met the criteria for participating in the help program, This will provide valuable support and opportunities for their development.



On the road show, We introduced our product - LDH based water purifier No. 1 and its supporting process to the judges. The product is based on the layered structure and ion exchange properties of hydrotalcite compounds, The surface area and ion transport rate are effectively improved, which further enhances the adsorption of dyes and ions in high-alkaline industrial printing and dyeing wastewater. Purification and separation efficiency of heavy metal ions and pollutants such as N and P. The newly developed LDH water purifier and its supporting process not only improve the recycling rate of materials, The treatment cost is reduced and the water quality index is significantly improved.



The leader of the team said that the qualification of the help is the result of the joint efforts of the team members. Over the past few months, Team members overcome various difficulties and challenges, constantly improve and adjust the project plan, This exciting result has finally been achieved. Access to the help plan will bring more resources and support to the team, Including financial, technical and market support. The team says they will continue to work to make the most of these resources, Constantly improve the project and strive for greater success.



The success of the team has also received attention and support from all walks of life. Many said they were proud of their team's achievements, And look forward to the team to achieve greater achievements in the future. The team will actively respond to the national ecological civilization construction policy, committed to the protection of water resources, and realize the virtuous recycling of water resources. The team said that they will continue to move forward with more enthusiasm and more firm faith, Strive to realize more dreams. In the New Year, a new starting point, the team will meet more challenges and create a more brilliant future with the support of the help plan!
